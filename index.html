About our Biryani.

Indulge in our Mutton Biryani, crafted with fresh, never-frozen, high-quality Zabiha Halal lamb leg meat, meticulously trimmed for leanness. Fragrant basmati rice, infused with the rich aroma of Desi ghee, creates a symphony of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. Please be aware that our dishes may contain nuts and dairy.

About us. We are KHAS.

Khaja's Hyderabadi Authentic'S biryani.

We're thrilled to announce that after careful consideration, we're excited to bring the authentic taste of Hyderabadi Biryani to Chicago! Like many of you, we've sampled biryanis from various places across the USA, especially in Chicago and its suburbs. After much anticipation, we believe now is the perfect moment to introduce the Nawabi-style Biryani that Chicago has been yearning for.

We understand that this may not be for everyone, but for those special individuals who miss the taste of Hyderabadi biryani from back home, this is a culinary delight not to be missed. Our head chef, Mohammed Khaja, has tirelessly experimented with hundreds of biryani variations to capture the essence of the OG Nawabi taste. Finally, after much dedication and refinement, he has perfected his secret recipe, bringing the traditional Hyderabadi biryani straight to the plates of everyone here in Chicago.
Get ready to indulge in the rich flavors and aromatic spices of Hyderabadi Biryani like never before! Stay tuned for updates on our launch date and where you can experience this culinary masterpiece firsthand.
